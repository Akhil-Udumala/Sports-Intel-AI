Access Control

Two roles.
Purpose-built access.

Firebase-powered two-tier role model — Admins manage coaches & monitor activity, Coaches analyse players and build squads for their assigned team.

Full platform control. Manage coach accounts, monitor activity logs, inspect any team's data, and access all analytics & predictions across both sports.

  • ◆ All player analysis & Deep AI predictions
  • ◆ Cricket & Football Best XI generation

Team-locked analysis access. Permanently assigned to one discipline and national team. Full analytics, predictions, comparison and Best XI for your squad.

  • ◆ Injury risk & fatigue probabilities
